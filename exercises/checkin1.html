My Passion for Snowboarding

How It All Started

My journey into snowboarding began during a high school field trip to Italy. I had skied as a child, but at some point, I knew I wanted to switch to snowboarding. It just felt more exciting, more thrilling, and looks cooler (I think)

Why I Love Snowboarding

Snowboarding is a sport that goes against human instincts. Think about it: you strap both feet onto a single board, making balancing and movement feel unnatural at first. But once you get the hang of it, the freedom is incredible.

  • Every little improvement feels amazing—jumping a bit higher, landing a new trick.
  • Challenging myself on harder trails keeps me motivated.
  • Riding with friends makes every trip unforgettable.
  • The speed, the rush, the wind—there’s nothing like it.

Tips for Beginners

If you're thinking of starting snowboarding, here are some tips:

  1. Start on easy slopes to build confidence.
  2. Learn the proper stance and how to balance.
  3. Take lessons or watch online tutorials.
  4. Always wear protective gear and layer up.
